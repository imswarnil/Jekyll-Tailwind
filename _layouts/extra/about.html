My Story

Hello! I'm Swarnil Singhai, a software engineer and content creator based in Bangalore, India. My world revolves around crafting elegant solutions with code and sharing the journey with a global community. I specialize in frontend development, particularly with React and Next.js, but my curiosity often leads me down the full-stack path.

My fascination with technology sparked during my university days. The power to build something from nothing, to solve real-world problems with logic and creativity, was captivating. After earning my Computer Science degree, I dived into the startup scene as a Junior Developer, cutting my teeth on JavaScript and the rapidly evolving frontend landscape.

In 2016, a new adventure began: my YouTube channel. It started as a way to document my learnings and help others navigate the complexities of web development. To my amazement, it blossomed into a vibrant community of over 500,000 aspiring and seasoned developers. Through tutorials, project deep-dives, and honest tech reviews, I aim to empower and inspire.

Professionally, I'm a Senior Software Engineer at TechCorp Inc., where I contribute to building scalable and user-centric enterprise applications. Beyond my day job, I'm passionate about creating comprehensive online courses and high-quality content that makes learning accessible and enjoyable.

When the screen is off, you'll find me exploring new tech horizons, contributing to open-source, capturing moments through my camera lens, or planning my next travel escapade. Life, for me, is a continuous loop of learning, creating, and sharing.
